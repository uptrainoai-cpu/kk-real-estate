Frequently Asked Questions

Kya property RERA approved hai?

Haan, sabhi projects Rajasthan RERA registered hain. Aap RERA website par verification kar sakte hain. Registration numbers available hain.

Home loan kitne time me approve hota hai?

Usually 7-10 working days me loan approve ho jata hai agar saare documents complete hain. Hamari team bank se coordination karegi.

Government subsidy kaise milegi?

PM Awas Yojana ke antargat first-time buyers ko ₹2.67L tak subsidy milti hai. Income criteria: ₹3-6L saalana. Ham help karenge.

Site visit free hai kya?

Bilkul free! Aapka convenient time lekar hum pickup arrange karte hain. Koi hidden charges nahi, koi obligation nahi.

Registry kab tak hoti hai?

Payment complete hone ke 15-30 days me registry ho jati hai. Saare legal documents clear hain, delay nahi hoga.
